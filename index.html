Smart Recipe Design

A case study by

Anika McPhee (UX Designer)

Faye Teodoro (UX Designer)

Vaishali Singh (UX Designer)

Hill Phatpanichot (UX Designer)

Conducted April-May 2023

The Design Challenge

Significant number of people struggle with finding new and exciting recipes that cater to their food preferences

The Solution

Creating AI-powered mobile application that generates personalized recipes for users based on their dietary preferences, available ingredients, and desired meal types.

Research Question

"What are the needs, preferences, and challenges users face about recipe discovery and meal planning?"

Reserach Objectives

Analyze user behavior's and challenges in meal planning.

Identify user preferences and dietary restrictions

Assess user satisfaction with existing recipe and meal-planning apps
